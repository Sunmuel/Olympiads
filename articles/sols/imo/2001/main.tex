\subsection{Problem 2.}
\begin{problem}{}{}
  Prove that for all positive real numbers $a,b,c$,\[ \frac{a}{\sqrt{a^2 + 8bc}} + \frac{b}{\sqrt{b^2 + 8ca}} + \frac{c}{\sqrt{c^2 + 8ab}} \geq 1.  \]
\end{problem}

By Hölder,
\[
  \left(\cyc \frac{a}{\sqrt{a^2+8bc}}\right)\left(\cyc a \sqrt{a^2+8bc}\right) \geq (a+b+c)^2.
\]
Hence, is it enough to prove that 
\[
  \displaystyle (a+b+c)^2 \geq \cyc a \sqrt{a^2+8bc} \iff 2(a^2b^2 +b^2c^2 + c^2a^2) \geq 2(a^2bc + ab^2c + abc^2),
\]
which is clearly true by the Muirhead's inequality.
